About Me

My career began with a fascination for structure and systems. Earning a PhD in Computational Chemistry taught me how complex processes can be understood, modelled, and improved. That analytical foundation evolved into a decade of leadership in ERP strategy, SaaS consulting, and data-driven transformation.

Today, I apply that same structured thinking to the world of AI and automation. I help organizations design efficient workflows, deploy intelligent tools, and align people with technology they can trust. Whether it’s automating ERP workflows, integrating AI copilots, or rethinking data pipelines, I focus on sustainable change, the kind that people adopt and organizations retain.

My approach remains evidence-driven and facilitative: I translate data and emerging technology into business impact executives can measure and teams can embrace.
