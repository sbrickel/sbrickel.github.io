Proof of Work

Case study: from research bottleneck to qualified pipeline

One full-time employee. 150 prospects researched per week. 10–12 outreach emails sent per month. That was the ceiling — not because the person was underperforming, but because manual research consumed everything.

Before

  • ~150 prospects researched per week, manually
  • No qualification or scoring
  • 10–12 personalised outreach emails per month
  • Employee consumed by research, not relationships

After

  • 25–50 qualified leads with draft emails per day
  • Automated scoring, classification, and CRM entry
  • 10–12+ personalised emails sendable per day
  • Employee focused on building relationships

A multi-agent AI system: automated prospect discovery, enrichment, qualification scoring, personalised email drafting, CRM integration, and a self-improving search loop. Designed, built, and delivered in under two weeks — with human review kept in the loop where judgement matters.

European e-commerce sector. Client details confidential.
