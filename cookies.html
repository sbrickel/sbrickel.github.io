Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies and similar technologies are used on sbrickel.github.io, why they are used, and how you can manage your preferences. It should be read alongside the Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow the site or third-party services to recognise your browser on subsequent visits. Some cookies are essential for the site to function; others are used for analytics or to provide embedded content.

This site does not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or behavioural profiling.

3. Cookie Categories

  • Strictly necessary: Required for the site to function. No consent is required under Spanish law (LSSI and AEPD guidance). These cannot be disabled without breaking site functionality.
  • Functional / third-party: Set by embedded third-party services (YouTube, Buy Me a Coffee, Tally). These require your consent under GDPR and the AEPD's 2024 cookie guidelines.
